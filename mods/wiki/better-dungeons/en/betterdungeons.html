Better Dungeons is a mod that allows you to open a world with a lot of challenges and rewards.
However, do not think that everything is so simple.
Each dungeon is guarded by a lot of monsters, which are not so easy to cope with, but if you still manage - you will get a lot of rewards.
Monsters roam the world even during the day, and the sky in this world seems dark green, but night vision reveals its true color - bright green.
